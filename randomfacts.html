Things I Like

I love to eat Gepuk Mahsuri almost every day without getting bored because it's my ultimate comfort food. The combination of spicy sambal and fried chicken just hits differently every single time. Besides that, I'm a big fan of iced milk tea drinks, and side dishes especially when I'm working on assignments or watching my favorite movies.

🎧

Music

Music is my best companion during study sessions and quiet nights. It helps me focus, calm my thoughts, and sometimes even lift my mood when I'm tired. My playlist changes depending on how I feel like sometimes upbeat pop songs, sometimes soft indie tunes, and occasionally sentimental ballads that remind me of special memories.

🌙

Habits

I'm most productive at night when everything feels peaceful and quiet. That's when ideas start flowing and I can concentrate better. I often end up opening too many tabs because I get curious about random things but that's how I learn and explore new ideas. Besides, I usually watch my favourite dramas before going to bed, hahaha!

💭

Dreams & Goals

Honestly, I just follow the flow of life without stressing too much about the future, but of course I dream of being successful and living comfortably one day. Deep down, I really hope to be financially stable and yes, I want to be rich! I also wish to travel and explore libraries or bookstores in other countries someday.

🎀

Random Fun Fact

I can spend hours reading random articles online about things I never knew I was interested in. One minute I'm reading about space, the next about ancient history or cute animals! My brain just loves collecting little bits of random knowledge.

In a Nutshell

Foodie, music lover, night owl, dreamer, and always curious about the world. That's me in a few words! I believe in enjoying the little things in life and surrounding myself with good people, good food, and good vibes always. 🌸
